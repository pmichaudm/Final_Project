ABOUT US

We are a small group of graduate chefs from the Tokyo Sushi Academy Tsukiji Branch who have come together to create Rolls for Days in order to bring our expertise and creativity directly to you. For us, sushi is not just food; it is also a work of art. We hope our food not only tastes like perfection, but also looks like perfection as soon as it is served to you.

WHAT'S NEW

With our restaurant continuously prospering, we are now offering combo meals for small (or large!) groups to enjoy together. Enjoy a diverse and savoury meal and choose from a selection of combos for everyone to try!

Our chefs are always experimenting with new recipes for rolls that will satisfy every person’s preferences. Be sure to stay tuned for future updates and new additions to the menu! Have suggestions? Feel free to forward your ideas over to your server, one of the chefs, or send us an email with your thoughts!
